Welcome!


Welcome to Rinella Produce, a food distribution company. We carry quality produce, canned and frozen goods, and much more at wholesale prices. While we deliver daily to restaurants, businesses and homes, individuals are welcome to visit the warehouse and hand-select their own produce at discounted prices. Produce orders- big or small- can be called-in and made ready for pick-up upon arrival.

"If you are looking for a place to find the best in fresh produce, as well as many other food items for sale, you need not look any further.
I could not give a higher recommendation than Rinella Produce, a place that excels in customer service, and has more than fair pricing on all their products."

- Yelp review from Troy F.

Our products include a full range of fresh produce, oils, meats, and frozen and canned goods. We also have an excellent prep crew on-site.

We are open to the public, so individuals may come in and pick out their fresh produce for home, or call ahead and have it ready when they come by!

Here at Rinella Produce our goal is to deliver quality produce at the lowest possible prices. We are always on the hunt to find alternatives for our customers; we want to help keep your food costs low.

Take a Look At Where We Deliver!

We deliver to the Portland Metro area six (6) days a week. We also have a delivery schedule for as far North as Longview, Washington; as far South as Eugene, Oregon; as far West as Seaside; as far East as Hood River. You can place your orders the night before- until midnight- for a next day delivery.
