Welcome to Rinella Produce, a food service provider and distribution company. Local and family-owned, we provide quality produce at wholesale prices. Our products include a full range of fresh produce, oils, eggs, meats, frozen and canned goods and much more. We have an excellent prep crew on-site to help cater to your individual needs. Delivery service is offered with a minimum order of $100. We also welcome individuals and businesses alike to visit our warehouse and hand-select their own produce at discounted prices. Orders &emdash; big or small &emdash; can be called-in and made ready for pick-up upon arrival. Our goal is to deliver quality products at the lowest possible prices. We pride ourselves on great service and are always on the hunt to find alternatives for our customers. We want to help keep your food costs low. Save some money &emdash; try Rinella!

Take a Look At Where We Deliver!

We deliver to the Portland Metro area six (6) days a week. We also have a delivery schedule for as far North as Longview, Washington; as far South as Eugene, Oregon; as far West as Seaside; as far East as Hood River. You can place your orders the night before- until midnight- for a next day delivery.
