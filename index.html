Welcome to Rinella Produce, a food distribution company. We carry quality produce, canned goods, frozen goods, and much more at wholesale prices. While we deliver daily to restaurants, businesses and retirement homes, individuals are more than welcome to come visit our warehouse and hand-select their own products at discounted prices. Any order — big or small — can be called in and made ready for pick-up upon arrival.

"If you are looking for a place to find the best in fresh produce, as well as many other food items for sale, you need not look any further.
I could not give a higher recommendation than Rinella Produce, a place that excels in customer service, and has more than fair pricing on all their products."

- Yelp review from Troy F.

Our goal is to deliver quality products at the lowest possible prices. We pride ourselves on great service, so we are always on the hunt to find alternatives for our customers. We want to help keep your food costs low, so save some money — try Rinella!

Take a Look At Where We Deliver!

We deliver to the Portland Metro area six (6) days a week. We also have a delivery schedule for as far North as Longview, Washington; as far South as Eugene, Oregon; as far West as Seaside; as far East as Hood River. You can place your orders the night before- until midnight- for a next day delivery.
