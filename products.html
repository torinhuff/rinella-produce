We try to simplify your shopping experience by offering a wide range of items. Our products include fresh produce, oils, proteins, frozen and canned goods and much more. We have an expert prep crew on-site to cater to your individual needs be it thinly sliced mushrooms for pizza, trimmed green beans for a catering event or peeled potatoes for home; we are here to help! Curious if we carry something? Just ask.

All of our fresh produce is thoroughly inspected upon arrival and again before your delivery. We want our customers to be happy with their purchase. If for some reason you are dissatisfied with part of your order, contact us within 48 hours and we will correct the problem right away. Rinella Produce means family, and just like family we will be here for you!
