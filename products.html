From fresh produce, eggs, and cheese to canned and frozen goods, we at Rinella Produce try our best to keep the items in stock that will limit your shopping needs. All of our produce is thoroughly inspected upon arrival and before delivery to ensure that our customers won't be disappointed with their purchases. If there is any problem with a product or an order we will try our best to let the customer know beforehand, and if upon delivery they aren't satisfied, we will give a full refund or exchange the product as soon as we possibly can. We want to make everyone's shopping easier, so if there is a product or item that isn't on our pricelist or in our warehouse — we will always try our best to find it for you at the best possible price. If you have any questions about what we carry or what we can get for you, please don't hesitate to give us a call!
