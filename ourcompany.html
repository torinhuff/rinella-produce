Our Company


Established in 1914 by current owner David Rinella’s grandfather, Frank Rinella, Rinella Produce has grown from a modest, one man door to door delivery business to an established company that provides quality food products to over 14 major cities in Oregon and Washington. Located within “Produce Row” in Southeast Portland, which consists of other major produce companies such as Pacific Coast Fruit, Graziano Foodservice, and Sheridan Fruit Company, we at Rinella have made our name by selling quality products with unmatched customer service. We may only deliver six days a week, but we have people on staff every single day to make sure your needs are met and you can trust what we bring to the table. From drivers who love to meet the chefs to salespeople who go above and beyond to make your order just right, Rinella Produce is a company that believes everyone is family, not just those who bear the name.



"If it costs you $10 or 15 bucks, or half a box of tomatoes, make it right."

- David Rinella, Owner and President of Rinella Produce

All of our products are inspected immediately upon arrival and before delivery, insuring that our customers will enjoy the products they receive. We understand the importance of fresh and clean produce, so if for any reason a customer isn't satisfied with what they've ordered, we're always willing to make things right. Give us a call and we'll make sure you get the quality you deserve. Want something that we don't carry? Don't stress! We're always willing to go out of our way to make sure we can find that product for you (even if it doesn't come directly from us). Rinella Produce means family, so just like family, we want to make sure everything is as good as possible on your end.
