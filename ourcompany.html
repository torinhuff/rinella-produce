Our Company


Established in 1914 by current owner David Rinella’s grandfather, Frank Rinella, Rinella Produce has grown from a modest, one man door to door delivery business to an established company that provides quality food products to over 14 major cities in Oregon and Washington. Located within “Produce Row” in Southeast Portland, we at Rinella have made our name by selling quality products with unmatched customer service. We may only deliver six days a week, but we have people on staff every single day to make sure your needs are met and you can trust what we bring to the table. From drivers who love to meet the chefs to salespeople who go above and beyond to make your order just right, Rinella Produce is a company that believes everyone is family, not just those who bear the name.



"If it costs you $10 or 15 bucks, or half a box of tomatoes, make it right."

- David Rinella, Owner and President of Rinella Produce

All of our products are inspected upon arrival to our facility and again before your delivery ensuring you will receive the best possible quality. If for some reason you are unhappy with part of your order, simply contact us within 48 hours and we will correct the problem right away. Your success is our mission. We understand the importance of having the right items for that lunch or dinner rush and we will do our best to assist you. Rinella Produce means family, and just like family we will be there for you!

Thank you for supporting us!
